Javascript Culture (Date/Number)

Press F12 to show console. Use `pt` variable for `pt-BR` or `en` for `en-US` language

Try:

  • pt.parseDate('26/8');
  • pt.parseDate('2008-02-25');
  • pt.format(new Date());
  • pt.format(new Date(), 'yyyy-MM-dd');
  • pt.format(new Date(), 'f');
  • pt.dateAdd('y', 2, new Date());
  • pt.dateDiff('M', pt.parseDate('01/01/2004'), pt.parseDate('25/04/2012'));
